How to Act Different and Alluring at School

Part 1 Part 2

Steps

Part 1:



  1. Look presentable. If you look like you're classy and carry yourself with admirable grace and style, people will admire you verbally behind your back.


  2. Talk politely. Use "I am sorry" and "Thank you" wherever need is required. Be soft-spoken, and smile when you talk to people. Also, make eye contact.


  3. Study well. Good grades will help enhance your charisma.

Part 2:



  1. Change your style from day to day. Try new hairstyles, new clothes, new shoes and new accessories.


  2. Compliment people. Few sweet words like, "You look nice today" or "Your hair today is awesome" or "Hey, I love your shoes" will make people like you better and they are most likely to admire you even more.


  3. Do not misbehave with anyone. Even when you smell a quarrel, avoid it. Stay silent, remember how you have to act. Impress people with your values and manners.

Tips

  • Set your personality. Don't be that someone who does what their friends do; be your own person.
  • Also, sometimes keep to yourself. But only sometimes. That way, you will appear mysterious, charming and very different.
  • Put on aromatic perfume, like lavender or cinnamon. Remember, you have to smell irresistibly good.
  • Look approachable, don't slouch, keep your posture and smile - Try not to talk too much, and be polite all the time.
  • Don't approach anyone to introduce yourself. However, when someone comes to you and you sense small talk, be very polite and talk like you're friends already.
  • Enhance your features. Use eyelash-curlers and kohl. And foundation and a little blush, if you'd like.

Warnings

  • Do not boast.
  • Don't ever speak loudly.
