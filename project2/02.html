Paul Eluard

LADY LOVE
She is standing on my lids And her hair is in mine

She is the form of my hands

And the color of my eyes, She is swallowed in my shadow Like a stone against the sky Her

eyes are always open

And she does not let me sleep In the light of day her dreams Make

suns evaporate,

Make me laugh, cry and laugh, And

speak

when I have nothing to

say.
